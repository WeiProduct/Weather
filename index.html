FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does AI Weather need an internet connection?

Yes. Weather data is fetched from weather providers, so a connection is needed to refresh current conditions and forecasts.

How is my location used?

Location is permission-based and used only to retrieve weather for your area. You can deny, change, or revoke access anytime in system Settings.

Can I track more than one city?

Yes. Add and manage multiple cities for home, travel, and family, and switch between them at any time.

Do I need to create an account?

No. AI Weather works without sign-up or account creation.

Which languages does AI Weather support?

AI Weather offers a bilingual interface in English and Simplified Chinese, and you can switch anytime.
